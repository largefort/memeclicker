Meme Origins & Evolution

1976

Term "Meme" Coined

Richard Dawkins introduces the term in "The Selfish Gene", describing cultural transmission similar to gene propagation.

1990s

Early Internet Memes

ASCII art and early forum jokes become precursors to modern memes.

2000s

Viral Image Macros

Websites like YTMND and 4chan popularize image-based memes.
